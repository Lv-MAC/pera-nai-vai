Common Questions (FAQ)

Can I send money between different services (e.g., bKash to Nagad)? +

Yes, this is now possible using the 'Binimoy' service, which you can find inside the bKash and Nagad apps. It allows you to transfer money between different MFS providers and banks. Be aware that Binimoy has its own fee structure, typically around 1% of the transaction amount, which is different from a regular 'Send Money' fee.

Who pays the fee when I 'Send Money'? +

The SENDER always pays the fee. If a 5 Taka fee applies for sending money, it will be deducted from the sender's account. The receiver gets the full amount that was sent.

What are the daily limits? +

As of March 2025, the unified limits set by Bangladesh Bank for most standard accounts are:

  • Cash Out (from Agent): 30,000 Taka per day / 200,000 Taka per month.
  • Send Money (P2P): 50,000 Taka per day / 300,000 Taka per month.

These limits apply across all major MFS providers (bKash, Nagad, Rocket).

Is bKash 'Send Money' always 5 Taka? +

No. bKash has a 'Priyo Number' feature. Sending money to up to 5 designated Priyo numbers is FREE for the first 25,000 Taka each month. The 5 Taka fee applies when sending to numbers that are not on your Priyo list. For simplicity, this calculator assumes the 5 Taka fee applies, but you can save money by using the Priyo feature!
